Itinerary

Ephesus & Ancient Wonders – Full Day Cultural Experience

A refined journey through classical history, designed for travelers seeking depth, comfort, and authenticity. Carefully operated by YFS Turizm.

Morning | Private Pick-Up & Introduction

Your experience begins with a scheduled pick-up from your hotel in a modern, air-conditioned vehicle. From the first moment, YFS Turizm ensures a smooth, unhurried, and comfortable start to the day.

During the journey, your licensed professional guide provides a comprehensive introduction to the historical significance of Ephesus, outlining its role as one of the most influential cities of the ancient world.

Ephesus Ancient City | Guided Exploration

Upon arrival, you enter the legendary Ephesus Ancient City, a remarkably preserved archaeological site reflecting the power and sophistication of Roman civilization. Walking along its marble streets, you will discover how daily life once unfolded in a city that hosted emperors, merchants, scholars, and philosophers.

  • Celsus Library – a symbol of knowledge and architectural excellence
  • Great Theatre – an extraordinary structure with a capacity of 25,000 spectators
  • Marble Road – the ceremonial axis of the ancient city
  • Temple of Hadrian – dedicated to imperial authority and Roman artistry
  • Agora – the commercial and social center of Ephesus
  • Ancient Baths & Fountains – showcasing advanced Roman engineering

Throughout the visit, your guide offers detailed explanations, allowing time for photography, observation, and personal exploration without rushing.

Midday | Lunch Break

A carefully timed lunch break is provided at a selected local restaurant. Guests may enjoy regional flavors and take time to relax before continuing the afternoon program. Lunch is optional and flexible according to guest preference.

House of Virgin Mary | Spiritual Heritage

The journey continues to the House of Virgin Mary, located in a serene natural setting on Mount Bülbül. Recognized as a sacred pilgrimage site, this location offers a calm and reflective atmosphere distinct from the archaeological intensity of Ephesus.

Visitors may spend quiet moments in contemplation while learning about the site’s importance within Christian tradition and world heritage.

Temple of Artemis | Ancient Wonder

The final historical stop is the Temple of Artemis, once counted among the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. Although only fragments remain today, the scale and influence of the temple are brought vividly to life through expert narration.

Afternoon | Return Journey

After a full day of cultural discovery, guests are comfortably transferred back to their hotel. The experience concludes with the assurance of YFS Turizm’s high service standards and attention to detail.

This itinerary is designed for travelers who value history, structure, and refined travel experiences.

FAQ

Ephesus & Ancient Wonders – Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to the most common questions before visiting Ephesus with YFS Turizm.

How long is the Ephesus tour?

Most Ephesus programs run as a full-day tour including transfers, guided walking time inside the ancient city, and selected nearby highlights depending on your route.

Where does pickup happen?

Pickup can be arranged from hotels in the area and from cruise ports. Your exact pickup time and meeting point are confirmed one day before the tour.

Is the tour suitable for cruise guests?

Yes. We plan timing around ship schedules and local conditions. We recommend an early start for the smoothest experience.

How much walking is involved?

Walking is moderate to high due to the size of the site and the stone pathways. Surfaces can be uneven and slippery when wet, so comfortable walking shoes are essential.

Is Ephesus accessible for strollers or wheelchairs?

Parts of Ephesus include uneven stones and slopes. We can adjust the route to reduce difficulty where possible, but full accessibility is limited at the archaeological site.

Are entrance tickets included?

Depending on the package, entrance fees may be included or optional. YFS Turizm confirms all inclusions clearly before you book.

What will we see inside Ephesus?

Key highlights typically include the Library of Celsus, the Great Theatre, Marble Road, the Agora, and major Roman-era streets and structures.

Is the House of Virgin Mary included?

It can be included as an optional stop depending on your route and timing. If it is important for your visit, tell us in advance so we prioritize it.

Do we visit the Temple of Artemis?

The Temple of Artemis is often offered as a short photo stop. It is a quick visit compared to Ephesus itself, but it is historically significant and can be included when timing allows.

What about lunch?

Lunch is usually scheduled after the main Ephesus visit so guests can rest. Restaurant choice is based on cleanliness, comfort, and consistent quality.

What should I bring?

Comfortable shoes, sun protection, and water are strongly recommended. In summer months, lighter clothing and earlier starts improve comfort.

When is the best season to visit?

Spring and autumn are the most comfortable seasons. Summer visits are possible with early timing to avoid peak heat. Winter can be quieter but may include rain.

Do you offer private Ephesus tours?

Yes. Private tours are available and recommended for families, couples, photographers, and travelers who want a flexible pace and customized stops.

Do you include shopping stops?

No forced shopping. If you want local crafts or a brief visit to a traditional workshop, it can be added by request — always optional, never pressure.

Can we combine Ephesus with other places?

Yes. Popular combinations include Ephesus + Şirince Village, Ephesus + Virgin Mary House, or a multi-day plan connecting Pamukkale or Istanbul flights.

How do we book with YFS Turizm?

Send your date, pickup location (hotel or port), and group size. We reply with clear options (standard and private) and confirm the final schedule.

Want exact price & availability?

Send dates + hotel area. We reply with clear options.
