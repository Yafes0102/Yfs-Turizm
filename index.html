Frequently asked

Frequently Asked Questions

General information about tours, reservations, and services provided by YFS Turizm.

Are you a licensed travel agency?

Thanks to our numerous sister agencies, we are able to conduct tours legally.

In which languages are tours available?

Our tours are primarily offered with professional English-speaking guides. Other language options may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Are hotel pick-up and drop-off included?

Most tours include hotel pick-up and drop-off from centrally located hotels. Details are clearly stated on each tour page.

Are entrance fees included in the tour price?

Entrance fees may or may not be included, depending on the selected package. Each tour page clearly lists what is included and what is optional.

Do you offer private or customized tours?

Yes. Private and customized tours are available for travelers who prefer a flexible schedule and personalized experience.

How do I make a reservation?

Reservations can be made easily via WhatsApp or email. Our team responds quickly with availability, options, and clear pricing.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary depending on the tour and service type. Exact cancellation terms are shared before confirmation.

Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is not included unless explicitly stated. Guests are advised to arrange their own insurance if required.

Are your tours suitable for children and elderly guests?

Most tours are suitable for families and senior travelers. Tours involving longer walking or stairs are clearly noted in advance.

How can I contact YFS Turizm during the tour?

Our team is available via WhatsApp for fast support before and during your tour.
